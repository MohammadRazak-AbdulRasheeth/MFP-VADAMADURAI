Your MFP Gym Management System is now a Progressive Web App (PWA) and can be installed directly on your mobile device, tablet, or desktop!

## Installation Instructions

### For iPhone/iPad (iOS)

1. **Open Safari** and navigate to your MFP Gym app URL
2. **Tap the Share button** (arrow pointing out of a box at the bottom)
3. **Scroll down** and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. **Give it a name** (e.g., "MFP Gym") and tap **"Add"**
5. The app will now appear on your home screen as a standalone app

### For Android Devices

#### Method 1: Chrome Browser (Recommended)
1. **Open Chrome** and navigate to your MFP Gym app URL
2. **Tap the menu button** (three dots at the top right)
3. **Tap "Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
4. **Confirm** the installation
5. The app will appear on your home screen

#### Method 2: Using the Install Button
1. Once the app loads, look for an **"Install App"** button that appears automatically
2. Tap it and confirm the installation
3. The app will be added to your device

### For Desktop/Laptop

1. **Open your web browser** and go to your MFP Gym app
2. **Look for the install icon** (usually in the address bar)
3. **Click "Install"** and confirm
4. The app will be installed as a standalone application

## Troubleshooting

### Install Button Not Appearing?
- Make sure you're using HTTPS (secure connection)
- Try refreshing the page
- Close and reopen the browser
- Try on a different browser

### App Not Installing?
- Clear your browser cache
- Update your browser to the latest version
- Try on a different WiFi network
- Restart your device

### Performance Issues?
- Clear the app's cache from Settings > Apps (Android) or Settings > General (iOS)
- Reinstall the app
- Make sure you have enough storage space

## How to Uninstall

### iOS
1. Long press the app icon on your home screen
2. Tap "Remove App"
3. Select "Remove from Home Screen"

### Android
1. Long press the app icon
2. Tap "Uninstall"
3. Confirm the uninstallation
